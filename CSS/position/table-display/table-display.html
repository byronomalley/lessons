Leo

The efforts of Home Grown Records is dedicated to Leo Carolan, a musician, a son, a brother and a friend who was tragically killed in an attempted robbery in 2016. The altercation had included one of Leo's friends, who narrowly escaped with a gapping stab wound to the body. Leo, having effectively saved his friends life, was murdered shortly thereafter. He is survived by his family and friends, and now he rests, with a record label in his honour.
